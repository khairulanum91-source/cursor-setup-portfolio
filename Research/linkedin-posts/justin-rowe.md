## Post 2
**Link:** [View Post](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/justin-rowe-4043339b_running-thought-leader-ads-doesnt-make-you-ugcPost-7448390680952901632-dYi2)

**Summary:**
- Emphasizes that tools alone (like thought leader ads) do not build authority
- Highlights the importance of strong content and credibility first
- Suggests ads should amplify proven content, not replace it

**Key Insight:**
> In market expansion, paid amplification only works when the 
> underlying message is already credible and valuable. Investing in 
> distribution before validating your content is a costly mistake.

## Post 3
**Link:** [View Post](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/justin-rowe-4043339b_linkedinads-impactable-ugcPost-7449514328728424449-Jap5)

**Summary:**
- Highlights the importance of building a full-funnel LinkedIn ads strategy
- Emphasizes combining organic content, paid ads, and retargeting
- Suggests multi-channel systems drive more consistent B2B growth

**Key Insight:**
> Sustainable B2B demand generation is not a single tactic — it is 
> a system. Organic content builds trust, paid ads expand reach, and 
> retargeting converts warm audiences into pipeline.
