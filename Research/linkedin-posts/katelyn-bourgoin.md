## Post 2

**Summary:**
- Explains that buyers prefer simple and easy-to-understand messaging
- Highlights cognitive ease as a key factor in decision-making
- Suggests simplifying communication to improve conversions

**Key Insight:**
> The brain defaults to the path of least resistance. In market 
> expansion, the company that communicates its value most clearly 
> and effortlessly will win attention and trust over a more capable 
> but harder-to-understand competitor.
